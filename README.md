# Insurance-Cost-Risk-Analysis-Dashboard (Power BI)

Project Overview : 
This project analyzes an insurance dataset to understand the key factors influencing medical charges using Power BI.

Objectives : 
* Identify factors affecting insurance costs
* Analyze the impact of smoking, BMI, and age
* Explore regional and risk-level variations

Tools Used : 
* Power BI
* Excel / CSV

Key Insights : 
* Smoking is the primary driver of high medical expenses, with smokers consistently incurring significantly higher charges across all age groups.
* There is a strong positive correlation between BMI and insurance costs; policyholders in the "Obese" category represent the highest financial risk.
* Insurance premiums exhibit a steady upward trend as age increases, reflecting the rising cost of healthcare services over time.
* A small segment of high-risk individuals (smokers with high BMI) contributes disproportionately to the total aggregate insurance expenditure.
* Regional analysis reveals notable fluctuations in average charges, suggesting that local healthcare costs and demographic habits influence pricing.

Conclusion :
Lifestyle and demographic factors significantly influence insurance costs. This dashboard helps in understanding these patterns for better decision-making.
